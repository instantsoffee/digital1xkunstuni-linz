COLORS

Colors are a vital part of web design, shaping the mood, guiding attention, and reinforcing brand identity. A thoughtful color palette enhances readability, accessibility, and emotional impact. When used effectively, colors can create visual harmony, improve user experience, and make a website more memorable and engaging.

Ultimately, color in web design is not just about aesthetics — it’s a powerful tool for communication. From signaling interactive elements to evoking emotions and guiding users through a page, color choices influence how people perceive and interact with a website. Understanding color theory, contrast, and accessibility standards is essential for creating designs that are both beautiful and inclusive.
